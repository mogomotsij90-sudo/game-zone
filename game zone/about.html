🎮 Our Story

GameZone Pula was founded in 2022 with a mission: bring the thrill of gaming to every corner of Botswana. From casual mobile players to hardcore console collectors, we offer authentic games, pre-owned gems, and the latest releases — all priced fairly in Pula. Our team tests every title and provides expert recommendations to help you choose the perfect game.

📍 Our Vision

Build the largest gaming community in Southern Africa, where players connect, trade, and level up together. We host monthly tournaments and support local esports.
